Our Policies

Our Policies

It really helps us better facilitate your appointment if you complete all required forms prior to arrival. Please carefully complete our medical forms and provide a complete medical history for your child. You can download, print and bring the required forms with you.

Appointments

Patient visits to Just 4 Kids Pediatric Clinic are by appointment; we accept walk-ins depending on the patient’s schedule. For scheduling purposes, every sibling will need an appointment to be seen by the provider. Appointments must be confirmed within 24 hours.

Cancelation policy

All cancelation/rescheduling appointments should be made 24 hours in advance. Late arrival policy: Patients arriving more than 15 minutes late may be asked to reschedule their appointment.

No-show Policy

If you do not call to notify us of either cancellation or tardiness, you will be considered a no-show. If you have three no-shows, you may be discharged from our clinic.

Parental Consent for Care Policy

All adults involved in the care of the child are encouraged to be part of office visits. However, any adult, NOT the child’s parent, must have written permission from the parent to bring the child in for medical care.
